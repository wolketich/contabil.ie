Questions you may find difficult to ask

How many years of Form 11 do I need?+

It depends on the lender. AIB commonly refers to two years and may request three in some cases. We follow your current checklist.

Can you guarantee mortgage approval?+

No. The bank assesses affordability, credit and the property. We prepare and explain the tax documents.

Can I apply with late returns?+

Speak with your broker, but your tax record may need to be brought up to date before declared income can be assessed.
