FAQ

Questions you may find difficult to ask

Why is my rate 35%?+

Revenue carries out rolling compliance reviews. Missing returns, outstanding balances or other issues can affect the rate.

Is deducted RCT lost?+

Not necessarily. Revenue credits deducted RCT to your tax record and may offset it against liabilities after a return is filed.

Can you change my rate to 0% immediately?+

No one can guarantee or set the rate except Revenue. We can correct identified issues and prepare your position for review.
