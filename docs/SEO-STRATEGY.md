# SEO strategy

## Site structure

Romanian pages live at the domain root because Romanian is the primary market. English uses `/en/`. Russian uses `/ru/`.

Every public page includes:

- a unique title and meta description
- one focused H1
- canonical URL
- Romanian, English, Russian and x-default hreflang links
- ProfessionalService structured data
- FAQ structured data on service-detail pages
- direct internal links to contact and related services

Each article should answer one search question fully, cite Revenue or a bank source, and lead to the relevant service page.

## Prohibited SEO practices

- keyword stuffing
- doorway pages with near-identical content
- hidden text
- fake reviews or aggregate ratings
- fabricated deadlines
- claims of guaranteed 0% RCT, tax refunds or mortgage approval
