# Claims register

This file records the basis for important public claims. Recheck sources before changing wording or publishing time-sensitive content.

## RCT rates

Claim: Current RCT rates are 0%, 20% and 35%.

Primary source: https://www.revenue.ie/en/self-assessment-and-self-employment/rct/index.aspx

Claim: Revenue carries out rolling compliance reviews and determines a subcontractor's RCT rate.

Primary source: https://www.revenue.ie/en/self-assessment-and-self-employment/rct/rct-subcontractors.aspx

Required disclaimer: Revenue determines the rate. Contabil.ie does not guarantee 0%.

## Late Form 11 filing

Claim: A late return can attract a 5% surcharge when filed within two months after the deadline, or 10% when more than two months late, subject to Revenue limits.

Primary source: https://www.revenue.ie/en/self-assessment-and-self-employment/guide-to-self-assessment/pay-file-system.aspx

Claim: The standard Form 11 deadline for the 2025 tax year is 31 October 2026.

Primary source: https://www.revenue.ie/en/self-assessment-and-self-employment/documents/form11-helpsheet.pdf

Do not claim that an online ROS extension exists until Revenue publishes the applicable extension.

## Tax registration

Claim: A sole trader's PPSN does not become their business Tax Reference Number until they register for tax.

Primary source: https://www.revenue.ie/en/starting-a-business/registering-for-tax/how-to-register-for-tax-as-a-sole-trader.aspx

## Mortgage documents

Claim: AIB describes Revenue-acknowledged Form 11 as a document required for a self-employed mortgage application.

Primary source: https://www.aib.ie/help-and-guidance/mortgages-faq/what-is-a-revenue-form-11

Claim: Bank of Ireland lists accounts, business bank statements and confirmation of tax affairs among documents for self-employed applicants.

Primary source: https://personalbanking.bankofireland.com/borrow/mortgages/documentation-checklist/

Required disclaimer: Document requirements vary and the lender decides mortgage approval. Contabil.ie prepares tax documents but cannot guarantee approval.
